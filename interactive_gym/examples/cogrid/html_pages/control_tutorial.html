In the next part of the tutorial, you will learn how to control the AI agent. Instead of completing the task yourself, you will have real-time controls over one AI agent while it completes the task with a second AI.

You will have discrete controls over two dimensions of the agent's behavior, as shown below:

Across each of these dimensions --- delivering dishes and putting onions in the pot --- you can encourage or discourage the agent from doing that action. This control allows you to manipulate the agent's behavior to suit your preferences for how you'd like them to complete the task and potentially how you want to complete the task yourself.

In this portion, you should gain some familiarity with these controls and how they affect the agent's behavior. In later rounds, you'll be able to use these controls to manipulate the agent's behavior to your liking while you complete the task with it.
