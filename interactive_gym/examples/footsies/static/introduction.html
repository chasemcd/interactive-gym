In this study, you'll be playing a simple fighting game against an computer-controlled opponent ("CPU"). Over the course of the study, you'll work to hone-in your skills and learn effective strategies. After a training phase, you'll be able to earn a bonus by beating the CPU in the "initial challenge" and "final challenge" rounds.

Here's how the study will progress.

  1. Before you begin, you'll fill our a brief survey.
  2. You'll first have an "initial challenge" with a set of 10 rounds against a CPU opponent. This is your first opportunity to earn a bonus. We'll ask a few brief questions once you've completed these rounds.
  3. Next, you'll practice the game for 30 rounds against a CPU training partner. This is not that same opponent that you will play in the initial or final challenge, but rather a partner that is meant to help you learn the game. The goal here is to become as skilled as possible so that you can earn a bonus in the final rounds. Once completed, you'll again fill out a brief survey.
  4. Finally, you'll play 10 rounds in our "final challenge" section of the study. This is your final opportunity to earn a bonus. We'll ask a few brief questions once you've completed these rounds.
  5. Before ending, you'll answer a few final questions about your experience with the game and your CPU opponents.

Your bonus will be based on your performance in the "initial challenge" and "final challenge" rounds. In total, you'll play 20 rounds of the game where you can earn a bonus. You should attempt to learn as much as you can from the training partner before the final rounds to build up your skills: each time you win in these rounds, you'll earn a bonus of $0.30. The maximum bonus you can earn is $6.00.

System Requirements

Please ensure your computer meets the following requirements before starting the study. Your internet connection must be stable enough to download the game and your computer must be able to run the game in your browser.

Component Minimum Requirement
Internet Connection Stable broadband connection (minimum 5 Mbps)
Web Browser Modern browser with WebGL support (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
Memory (RAM) 4 GB RAM minimum
Graphics Any GPU with WebGL support
