Footsies is a simplified 2D fighting game that focuses on the fundamental aspects of combat. With just basic movement and a single attack button, players must carefully manage space and predict their opponent's actions to win.

Controls

You will control the fighter on the left hand side of the screen.

The controls are simple: use the and keys to move your character, and press the space bar to perform an attack.
Here are the special moves you can perform in addition to the basic attacks:

  • Forward Dash: Double tap quickly
  • Backward Dash: Double tap quickly
  • Special Attack: Hold space bar for at least 1 second (while standing still or moving forward), then release to attack

When you are ready to begin to play, press continue. You will first play against a training partner before you attempt to beat the AI to earn your bonus.

If the game does not immediatelyappear, please wait a moment while it loads.

When the game loads on the next screen, press vs. cpu to begin.
